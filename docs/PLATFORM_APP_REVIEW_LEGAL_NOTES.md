# Platform App Review Legal Notes

These notes support resubmission for TikTok, YouTube/Google, and Instagram/Meta review. They are operational notes, not legal advice.

## TikTok Reviewer Note

The Privacy Policy and Terms of Service were updated and are now published on the official website:

- Privacy Policy: https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/privacy
- Terms of Service: https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/terms
- User Data Deletion: https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/data-deletion

The updated documents explain what TikTok data is collected, why each TikTok scope is used, how data is stored, how long data is retained, how users can request deletion, and how users can disconnect TikTok or revoke access. The Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and User Data Deletion links are active and visible on the official website without requiring users to open a hidden menu.

The app only requests the TikTok scopes needed for its stated functionality: `user.info.basic` to identify the connected TikTok account, `video.upload` to prepare user-selected videos, and `video.publish` to publish user-selected videos when the user launches a campaign.

## Compliance Checklist Before Resubmission

- Privacy Policy URL is active: `https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/privacy`.
- Terms of Service URL is active: `https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/terms`.
- User Data Deletion URL is active: `https://www.plataformmultipublisher.com/data-deletion`.
- Legal links are visible on the official website without requiring users to open a hidden menu.
- The website is not only a login page or landing page; it explains the product, supported platforms, workflow, and legal documents.
- TikTok scopes match the app functionality shown in the product and review notes.
- Demo video shows the full TikTok authorization flow, connected account state, campaign creation, selected TikTok destination, publishing action, and status result.
- App review notes explain every TikTok product and scope used.
- URL ownership verification is completed if required by TikTok.
- YouTube OAuth consent screen points to the published Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
- YouTube/Google scopes shown in the OAuth consent screen match the actual app functionality.
- Instagram/Meta app settings point to the published Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
- Instagram/Meta review notes explain use of `instagram_business_basic` and `instagram_business_content_publish`.
- Confirm the filled legal details are still accurate before final submission: company/developer name, contact email, address, countries, retention period, service providers, hosting details, age limit, liability cap, and governing law.
- Have the final documents reviewed by a qualified legal professional before publishing or relying on them for platform approval.
